Description

Set in a abandoned museum, the human survivors have treaded upon this location in search of the legendary Sword of Absolut Infinity. Little did they know about the evil that lurks within the museum. Now, they have to retrieve the sword and get out before they are trapped there forever.

New Cooperative mode!

Once again, the survivors found themselves at a mysterious place where the monsters roams freely. Over the years, these monsters have developed a new trait which allows them to turn invisible. Luckily, one of the survivor has the ability to reveal hidden monster. With the other human survivor armed with the legendary Sword of Absolut Infinity, they would have to work together to get out of this alive.

Characters

HUMAN

Human survivor are highly vunerable and a single attack from the monsters is enough to take them down.

Eye power

Stop monster in their track when seen

Slayer

Kills monster (only available when holding Sword of Absolute Infinity)

Fourth Eye Cooperative mode only

Reveals hidden monsters and make them vunerable to physical attacks. (Only available to Spotter)

Monster

Evolved for hunting, these monsters are fast and merciless. Only the Sword of Absolute Infinity is able to hurt them.

Petrify

Turn human into stone upon contact

Blend

Able to disguise themselves with the environment

Wanderer

Moves faster than humans
